Profile

Professional Overview

Dr. Deena Nath Gupta is an accomplished academic and researcher with over 8 years of combined experience in teaching, industry research, and institutional development. Currently serving as Associate Professor at Maharishi University of Information Technology, Noida, he specialises in Information Security, IoT, Lightweight Cryptography, Machine Learning, and Blockchain.

He previously contributed as a Senior Research Analyst at CDAC-Mumbai (Ministry of Electronics & IT, Govt. of India). He holds a Ph.D. from Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI), New Delhi, has filed a patent (published January 2024), authored a textbook, and was invited as External Session Chair at ICCSC-2026, technically co-sponsored by IEEE.
