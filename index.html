About

Professional Summary

Dr. Deena Nath Gupta is a distinguished academic and researcher with over 8 years of integrated experience spanning teaching, applied research, and institutional development. Currently serving as Associate Professor at Maharishi University of Information Technology, Noida, he has built a strong scholarly reputation at the intersection of Information Security, IoT, Lightweight Cryptography, and Machine Learning.


In his recent capacity as Senior Research Analyst at CDAC-Mumbai (Centre for Development of Advanced Computing, Ministry of Electronics & IT, Government of India), Dr. Gupta contributed to cutting-edge national-level technology initiatives. His research portfolio spans patent filings, peer-reviewed publications in high-impact journals, and authorship of academic textbooks — demonstrating a rare blend of theoretical depth and practical impact.
